Logo Rules · Do's & Don'ts
Do
Minimum Clear Space
Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "G" crossbar on all sides.
Don't
Gold on Gold
Never place the gold logo on gold-tinted backgrounds. This destroys contrast.
Do
Minimum Size: 24px
Never render smaller than 24×24px on screen or 8mm in print.
Don't
Rotate or Distort
Never rotate, stretch, skew, or add effects. Always 0° rotation.
Typography · 3 Typeface System

Familjen Grotesk for headlines — a distinctive geometric grotesk with warm character and weights up to 700. Gaist for body text — clean, readable, and pairs naturally with Familjen Grotesk's geometry. Space Mono for system labels, metadata, and code. Note: Gaist requires a commercial license; previews below use system sans-serif as fallback.
