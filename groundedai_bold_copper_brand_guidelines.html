Why Bold Copper

Bold Copper #C46A2D communicates industrial precision, metallic energy, and engineering rigour. It evokes extracted ore, heavy machinery, and processed metal — exactly the world GroundedAI operates in. Unlike the earthy variant (#A0614A), Bold Copper has the saturation to cut through dark UI backgrounds and create the visual punch a technical platform needs.

Do
CTAs and action elements
Bold Copper excels as button backgrounds (white text), hover borders, active indicators, and 3px accent bars. High-impact, small surface area.
Eyebrow LabelSection Title
Do
Eyebrows, labels, accent words
Use Copper Deep (#9A5223) for text on white. Copper Light (#D4894E) for text on dark. Bold Copper for single accent words in headings.
Large copper background fill
Don't
Large background fills
Bold Copper should not fill entire sections, heroes, or card backgrounds. Its strength is in concentrated, purposeful application — not as a dominant surface.
This is a paragraph of body text rendered entirely in Bold Copper, which creates eye strain and poor readability.
Don't
Body text in Copper
Copper is not a body text colour. Use Warm Brown 60% on light and Cream on dark. Reserve Copper for structural elements and interactive states only.
